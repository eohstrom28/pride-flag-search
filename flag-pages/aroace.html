Aromantic asexual

Definiton

Disclaimer: People can experience identities in different ways. As such, people can identify with a label without exactly matching the definition because identities are fluid.

Aromantic asexual (often shortened to "aroace") is a term used by people who are both aromantic and asexual, or are on both the aromantic and asexual spectrums. Asexual people may still experience other forms of attraction and can be any gender; the term only refers to a person's sexual and romantic orientation. Aromantic asexual is an umbrella term for identities that are part of both the aromantic and asexual spectrum (see more in the Related Identities section).

Some aromantic asexual people date and/or pursue other types of relationships (such as queerplatonic or platonic ones).
